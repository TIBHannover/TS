Welcome to the Central Portal of TIB Terminology Services

A [[${title}]] is a repository for terminological resources based on semantic W3C standards like the Web Ontology Language (OWL 2). The [[${title}]] of TIB – Leibniz Information Centre for Science and Technology and University Library is a single-point-of-entry web-service providing access to terms from generic and domain-specific controlled vocabularies and ontologies. [[${org}]] Central [[${title}]] is the backbone of all the Terminology Services presented by [[${org}]]. It hosts and stores the terminologies of various research communities mainly through this web site.

Community Terminology Services

TIB classifies terminologies with respect to certain subject classification schemas and the needs of several research communities. In this context, it maintains additional Terminology Services of the following research communities as extensions to this service:

  • Engineering Terminologies in:
  • Chemistry Terminologies in:
  • Economics Terminologies in:
Ontologies that belong to these research communities can also be filtered from this service. It is planned to host more community specific Terminology Services as extensions of the central service.

Functionalities

In further stages, the service will be expanded by the following functionalities:

  • reliable storage for newly created domain-specific vocabularies,
  • collaborative creation of ontologies including a governance structure, a versioning system, a management platform and the possibility to identify missing terms and make term suggestions
  • a help-desk to assist the user community in the curation of terminologies.

The technical components of the service are based on open source solutions. This service is based on the Ontology Look-up Service (OLS) developed by the Samples, Phenotypes and Ontologies Team (SPOT) at the European Bioinformatics Institute (EMBL-EBI). Further modules of the service could be realized by other open source tools such as GitLab, Web-Protégé, or Redmine.

About the Ontology Lookup Service

The Ontology Lookup Service (OLS) is a repository for biomedical ontologies that aims to provide a single point of access to the latest ontology versions. The user can browse the ontologies through the website as well as programmatically via the OLS API. OLS is developed and maintained by the Samples, Phenotypes and Ontologies team at EMBL-EBI.

Funding

OLS is supported in part by CORBEL funded by the EU's Horizon 2020 research and innovation programme (2014–2020) under grant agreement number 654248 and by DIACHRON, EU FP7 Capacities Specific Programme,/grant/agreement/number/284209.
